Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the causes of heart disease?

    High Blood Pressure, Obesity, Smoking, Alcohol drinking, Lack of Exercise, Unhealthy Food etc.

  • How to prevent Heart Disease?

    Control on Smoking, Diabetics, Alcohol Drinking, Eating Healthy Food, Regular Exercise etc.

  • What are the symptoms of heart disease?

    Symptoms can vary depending on the type of heart disease but may include chest pain, shortness of breath, fatigue, dizziness, fainting, swelling in the legs, rapid or irregular heartbeat, and nausea.

  • What are the treatment options for heart disease? .

    Treatment varies depending on the specific type and severity of heart disease but may include lifestyle changes, medications (such as statins, blood thinners, or blood pressure-lowering drugs), medical procedures (like angioplasty or bypass surgery), implantable devices (such as pacemakers or defibrillators), or cardiac rehabilitation programs.
